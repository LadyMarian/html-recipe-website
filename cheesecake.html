Cheesecake that melts in your mouth


The best cheesecake is the one that is solid but still creamy. Not too much like cake, but also not pudding. It needs to melt in your mouth and be deliciously sweet. This is the one!

Ingredients

Bottom

  • 200 gr. biscuits
  • 75 gr. butter

Cheesecake

  • 1 kg creamcheese
  • 125 gr sour cream
  • 250 gr white caster sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 40 gr. flour
  • 1 vanilla pod, flesh
  • 1/2 lemon shavings
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Preparation

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 175℃.
  2. Crush the biscuits and mix it with the melted butter.
  3. Spread this evenly over the bottom of the baking mold, and press with the convex side of a spoon.
  4. Bake this for 1 minutes and let it cool down completely afterwards.
  5. Lower the ove temperature to 130℃.
  6. Butter the sides of the baking mold.
  7. Mix all the ingredients for the cheesecake to an even masses and spread it over the bottom.
  8. Bake it in the oven for 90 - 120 minutes. The cake needs to be solidified but may jiggle a bit in the middle.
  9. Let the cake cool down to room-temperature and store it overnight in the fridge.
  10. Enjoy!
