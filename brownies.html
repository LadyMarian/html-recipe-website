Brownies from Chocolate heaven


Nine out of ten people love chocolate. The tenth always lies. Here is the ultimate recipe to make the most delicous brownies to enjoy with your friends or just to keep all to yourself.

Ingredients

  • 225 gr. butter
  • 115 gr. dark chocolate with at least 78% cocoa
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 330 gr. sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 125 gr flour
  • 25 gr. cocoa powder

Preparation

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 175℃
  2. Butter up the baking mold
  3. Put the butter and chocolate in a heat-resistant bowl and melt au bain-marie
  4. Let the melted substance cool down a bit
  5. Whisk the 2 eggs and egg youlk together with the sugar, salt and vanilla extract, till it's light-colored and creamy (approximately 5 minutes at high speed)
  6. Carefully mix the chocolate mixture through the egg mixture with a spatula
  7. Carefully mix the flour and cocoa powder into the batter
  8. Pour the batter in the baking mold
  9. Place t in the oven for 40 minutes
  10. Let it cool down before cutting the brownies in pieces
  11. Enjoy!
