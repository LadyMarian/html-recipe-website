Viva Italia, the real pizza


The only place to get the real Italian pizza's, just the way they are meant to be, is in Italy. They are thin, with a firm crust with some bubbles in it. Now you can make them exactly like the original in your own home. Impress all your friends with this real Italian snack. This recipe is for making 3 pizza's

Ingredients

Bottom

  • 300 gr. patent flour
  • 7 gr. dry yeast
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 10 gr. salt
  • 150 ml water

Topping

  • Passata di pomodoro
  • Fresh basil
  • grated mozzarella
  • any other ingredients you like

Preparation

  1. Mix the ingredients for the dough into a breadmaking machine and use the setting for making dough.
  2. Preheat the oven to 270℃.
  3. Divide the dough into 3 balls and roll out the balls to very thin circles. Use some flour so it won't stick to your surface or the rolling pin.
  4. Spread a thin layer of passata on the pizza bottoms.
  5. Spread a thin layer of grated mozzarella on top of the passata.
  6. Cut a few basil leaves to small pieces and spread them over the pizza's
  7. Add some other ingredients you like, but remember: "less is more". The real Italian pizza's don't have excessive toppings.
  8. Bake the pizza's in the oven for 7-8 minutes, until the cheese turns golden and bubbles.
  9. Enjoy!
